# Round 1 advice — stats lens

Method: lock the result direction off scoring/conceding profiles + recent form, then take the
statistically-modal scoreline (even when chalk). Goals derived from each side's GF/g and GA/g
against the specific matchup quality; condition notes only where they move the expected goals.

- Confidence: high
- Why (through the stats lens): Mexico 2.0 GF/g, 0.9 GA/g, 8-match unbeaten; South Africa a 1.5 GF/g sea-level squad at 2240m altitude in front of 87k — clean-sheet-leaning Mexico opener, 2-0 is the modal home win.
