# Round 1 advice — contrarian lens

Lens discipline: lock the defensible toto to bank the 60, then chase the underpriced *scoreline shape* for the +30. Where the stats genuinely support a draw/upset the crowd dismisses (calibration permits flipping the toto only when stats favour it), I take it — this is the boldest of the three voices.

## Mexico vs South Africa @ Mexico City (alt ~2240m): 2-0
- Toto: home win
- Confidence: med
- Why (through the contrarian lens): Crowd wants Mexico 2-1 or 3-1; but Azteca altitude saps a wholly sea-level South African squad (Foster/Williams both fitness doubts) into the third quarter, and Mexico (GA/g 0.9, ~40% clean sheets) shut down a side that drew Jamaica/Panama — a clean 2-0 is the underpriced shape vs the goal-fest line.
