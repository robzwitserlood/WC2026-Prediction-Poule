# QF advice — conditions-market lens

_Stage multiplier 2× (toto 120 / exact 180). Lens: weight venue / heat / travel / fatigue and the
bookmaker-vs-profile mispricing gap above all else. Two of four ties carry a real conditions lever
(M98, M99); the other two are neutral-weather, so I read them off the market gap alone. Scorecard
note: my high-confidence calls carry (cum. high 79%), my med bucket is coin-flip — so I reserve `high`
for the one tie where the conditions edge and the statistical profile point the same way, and stay
disciplined (`med`) elsewhere._
