# Round 2 advice — contrarian lens

Method: lock the most defensible toto (protect the 60), then chase the market-underrated *defensible* scoreline within it (the +30). I commit to value: where the stats genuinely support a defensible upset/draw the crowd dismisses, I take it — and where the toto is chalk-locked, I push the scoreline shape (margin, BTTS, clean sheet) the crowd won't pick. Standings context: 0-pt sides (South Africa, Czechia, Qatar, NZ, Curaçao, Saudi, Iraq, Senegal, Jordan, DR Congo, Croatia, Australia) must chase — that opens up goals and BTTS shapes the crowd underprices.
