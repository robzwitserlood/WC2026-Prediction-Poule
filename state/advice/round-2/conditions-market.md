# Round 2 advice — conditions-market lens

Lens rule: weight venue (altitude / heat / humidity / indoor-AC), travel & acclimatisation, host advantage, and the bookmaker-vs-stats mispricing gap **above** raw talent. I flag where the crowd overrates cool-climate squads in hot/high spots, and where market price diverges from the team's statistical profile. Each pick states the condition delta vs the naive (indoor, neutral-venue) baseline.
