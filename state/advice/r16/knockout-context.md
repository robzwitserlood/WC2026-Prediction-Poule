# R16 advice — knockout-context lens

_One blind voice in the 4-lens panel. Stage R16 (×1.5: 90 toto / 135 exact). Penalties decide advancement but do NOT score — a level-after-120 tie is a real scoreable draw, and naming the shootout winner is this lens's job. Lens: suspensions/fatigue, PSO & extra-time temperament (Tier 2), big-game tactics/experience, manager KO record, squad depth for 120 min._

_Calibration discipline (R32 self-review): high-confidence calls hit 4/4, med/low were mispriced. So I reserve HIGH only where the temperament/suspension signal genuinely discriminates, and drop to MED/LOW where the read is real but the toto is coin-flippy. Fresh R32 evidence cuts both ways: Germany (6-1 all-time PSO) and Netherlands both went OUT on penalties — pedigree is not armour — but Croatia's shootout thesis never got tested (won in 90). Net lesson: trust the CURRENT squad's PSO tools (a specific hero keeper, a proven clutch spine) over the dusty nation-badge record._
