# R16 advice — stats lens

_Method: predict-knockout / round-advisor. Knockout football is cagier and lower-scoring than group output, so goal totals are shaded down from the group profiles. Toto (result direction) locked first to protect the 90 (R16 is 1.5×: 90 toto / 135 exact); exact shape chosen within it for the +45. For any 120-minute draw I name the shootout winner (advancement only, unscored). Base rates used: R16 favourite-advance ~68-70%, ET ~38%, PSO ~21% (Tier 1). Every call grounded in the cited team-file statistics; this is the disciplined baseline, no hedging toward the other lenses._
