## Matchup notes
- System: Rangnick 4-2-3-1 with very high defensive line and intense gegenpressing. Counter-press within seconds of ball loss. Quick vertical transitions.
- Collective output exceeds individual talent — institutional pressing system. Whole is greater than sum of parts.
- Defensive line vulnerability: high line vs. pacey forwards (Messi's through-balls for Lautaro, Amoura's runs) could be punished.
- Set pieces: Alaba/Sabitzer delivery. Alaba DEF goal on corners is a genuine threat (market underprices DEF scorers at Scorito rates).
- Conditions: Predominantly German Bundesliga-based squad — adapted to cool/moderate European conditions. 1. All the headline qualifying numbers include 10-0 vs San Marino — strip that and the attacking output is much more modest.
2. Alaba returning from major knee injury after missing Euro 2024 — if he's not 100%, the defensive structure loses its organising presence and set-piece delivery quality drops.
3. High-line gegenpressing in 30°C+ US heat (possible Kansas City/Atlanta venue) could be neutralised by a counter-attacking Algeria with the pace of Amoura.
4. Group win odds at 19% imply reasonable pricing. But if you're looking for value in the group, **Algeria +1 vs Austria** or a draw in their Group J decider (Jun 26) is probably correctly priced or slightly +EV.
