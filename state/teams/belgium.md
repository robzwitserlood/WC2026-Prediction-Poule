## Matchup notes
- System: 4-3-3 / 4-2-3-1 with wide pace (Doku, Fofana) and De Bruyne as controller.
- Press: medium-high press, likes to dominate possession.
- Vulnerable: right side defence has been exposed; Wales scored 5 in qualifying total.
- Set pieces: very dangerous — De Bruyne deliveries, Tielemans late runs, Vertonghen-era aerial presence now gone (generational shift).
- Heat/humidity: most squad members based in Premier League / Serie A / Bundesliga (temperate climates). US summer heat (Seattle, LA venues) may marginally affect.
- Golden generation's final window — squad motivation high but several key veterans are compromised physically.

USA reached R16 via a 2-0 win over Bosnia but will be **without Folarin Balogun (red card, one-match ban)**.
