## Mispricing flag
South Korea at +310 to win the group is interesting — their qualifying stats are elite (40 goals, 8 conceded) but against AFC opposition, not Europe. The 0-5 thrashing by Brazil and defeats to Ivory Coast and Austria expose a ceiling. However, the market may be **underrating their tactical flexibility and Son's big-game experience** relative to Czechia at +320. Both Czechia and South Korea are priced essentially equal to win the group, but South Korea's qualifying performance was demonstrably stronger and their squad depth better. **Czechia at +320 looks overpriced relative to South Korea (+310) — they are essentially equivalent odds for a less credentialed side.** Son's record at big tournaments historically outperforms his recent club form.
