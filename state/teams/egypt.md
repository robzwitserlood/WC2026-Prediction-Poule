## Matchup notes
- System: 4-2-3-1 / 3-4-1-2, compact and disciplined mid-low block.
- Counter-attack pace through Salah on the right and Marmoush as second striker.
- Defensive solidity is elite at this level: 2 GA in 10 qualifying matches; near-impossible to break down in low-block.
- Set pieces: Salah corners and free kicks are a primary weapon; Abdelmaguid offers aerial threat.
- Heat/humidity: Salah plays Premier League (UK climate) but Egyptian players accustomed to heat at home; should adapt better than most European opponents at Seattle / US venues.
- Travel/schedule: Egypt plays Seattle (vs Belgium Jun 15) → Vancouver (vs NZ Jun 21) → unknown (vs Iran Jun 27). Travel fatigue a minor concern.
