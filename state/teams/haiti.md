The Scotland match (Jun 14) is the sole realistic points opportunity. Scotland returns to WC after 28 years, never reached knockouts, lost Gilmour (key CM). The market consensus (Haiti +10000) implies near-zero chance — but in a single match against a historically underachieving Scotland side, Haiti beating Scotland is 25–30% likely by reasonable modeling. The Haiti-wins-Scotland scoreline could be a live value pick.
