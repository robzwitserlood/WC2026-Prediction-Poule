## Mispricing flag
Qatar at +3500 is almost certainly correctly priced as a very heavy underdog. Their 2022 WC record was the worst ever by a host nation. Under Lopetegui, the recent 0W in 5 (1 goal scored, 6 conceded) is alarming. However, there are two potential angles:
1. **Qatar's heat/conditions advantage in Group B is real but crowded by knowledge** — Seattle and Vancouver are not hot enough to create a genuine edge.
2. **Almoez Ali at +3500 team odds might conceal individual goal threat** — he has 60 international goals and 12 AFC qualifying goals; he WILL score chances if created. **If Afif's creative platform produces chances, Almoez Ali can convert** — the market prices Qatar near-zero but he is a dangerous finisher against weak defenses.
3. **Genuine value watch**: Qatar to score at least 1 goal per game may be underpriced at current group odds. They are not a 0-goal team on their history; Lopetegui's defensive structure may stabilize this squad.
