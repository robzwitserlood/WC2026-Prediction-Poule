## Matchup notes
- System: 4-3-3 (vertical) under Pape Bouna Thiaw. High press, counter-attack through Mané/Sarr pace. Deep defensive block available.
- Defensive strength: Koulibaly + Moussa Niakhate CB pairing provides aerial dominance and experience. 7 clean sheets in 10 WCQ games.
- Weakness: Attacking fullbacks push very high, leaving wide spaces for pacey counter-attacks. Aggressive press exposes rest defense.
- Set pieces: Mané on penalties and key delivery. Koulibaly an aerial threat on corners — DEF scoring potential (Scorito-relevant: DEF goal = 4× striker).
