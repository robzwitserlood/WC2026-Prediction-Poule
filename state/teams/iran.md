## Squad signals (as of 2026-06-10)

- Taremi: fit and in form after solid Olympiacos season. Carries enormous goalscoring burden alone now.
- Domestic league suspension: players based in Iran lacked competitive football for ~3.5 months. Fitness levels of domestically-based squad members uncertain.
- Coach: Amir Ghalenoei.
- Base: Tijuana (Mexico) — arrived after behind-closed-doors Turkey friendlies due to US-Iran political tensions around travel logistics.

## Matchup notes
- System: 4-3-3 / 4-2-3-1 pragmatic low-to-mid block; counter-attacking on the break.
- Defend deep, compress space, hit on transitions through Taremi.
- Vulnerable to pace behind a high line (exposed vs Portugal and England at WC2022).
- Set pieces: both a weapon (Taremi, Jahanbakhsh delivery) and a vulnerability when defending aerial balls.
- Heat/humidity: Tijuana base is relatively temperate; Iranian domestic players may be less acclimatised to US summer heat.
- Geopolitical travel: playing base in Mexico rather than US creates logistical overhead vs opponents staying in the US.
- Group G opener: vs New Zealand (Jun 15) — should win; Belgium (Jun 21) and Egypt (Jun 27) are the hard matches.
