Haaland is in career-peak form (27 PL goals, UCL top scorer). The 28-year WC absence from the market narrative means casual betters anchor Norway as a perennial non-qualifier — bookmakers haven't fully priced the structural improvement. The Opta 82.3% R32 chance at 28/1 implies ~3.6% win probability — likely underestimates the ceiling of a Haaland-led side in a favourable group.

Key specific value: **Norway to top Group I** (currently priced as second favourites behind France) is worth examining. France managing Mbappe/Saliba in early rounds + Deschamps conservatism vs. Norway's attacking firepower makes a Norway group win more than 25% likely.

Risk: Odegaard fitness. If he misses games, Norway's chance creation drops significantly and the value case weakens. Defensively, the high line vs Mbappe/Mane pace is a genuine vulnerability.
